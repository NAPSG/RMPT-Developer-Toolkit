Zoom Slider widget

The Zoom Slider widget provides interactive zoom controls in the map display. Hover over the Zoom Slider widget and click the show or hide button to show or hide this widget on the map.

Configure the Zoom Slider widget

No configuration is needed for the Zoom Slider widget.

Use the Zoom Slider widget

Click the zoom slider button to zoom in or out on the map.
